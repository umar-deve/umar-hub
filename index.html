My Story

My Story: Passion Meets Innovation

Bells University Student

Usman Umar

Tech Entrepreneur & Digital Solution Provider

Driven by Curiosity

Growing up with limited resources taught me resilience, creativity, and the value of hard work. From an early age, I developed a strong passion for technology and problem-solving, always eager to explore how things work and how ideas can be turned into real solutions.

Who I Am

I'm Usman Umar, a 200-level student at Bells University, Ogun State, with roots in the vibrant streets of Lagos. My journey from Lagos' bustling neighborhoods to university life has fueled my passion for making technology accessible and affordable. I combine academic learning with practical experience to provide tech solutions that actually work for students and small businesses. As a fellow student, I understand tight budgets and tight deadlines—that's why I offer services that deliver real value without the premium price tag.

Ambition & Goals

My ambition is to become a leading tech solutions provider in Nigeria, bridging the digital divide for students and small businesses. I aim to create affordable, accessible technology services that empower others to achieve their goals, just as I'm working to achieve mine.

Long-term, I envision building a tech hub that provides training, resources, and opportunities for young Nigerians from similar backgrounds, creating a pathway from the streets to sustainable tech careers.
